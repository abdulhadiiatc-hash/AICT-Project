Visa & Application Info

Everything you need to know before you apply

Required Documents for Hajj / Umrah Visa

  • Valid Pakistani passport (at least 6 months validity remaining)
  • CNIC / B-Form copy for all travellers
  • Passport-size photographs (white background)
  • Polio vaccination certificate
  • Meningitis vaccination certificate
  • Proof of relationship (for families / mahram)
  • Bank statement showing sufficient funds

How to Apply

  • Step 1 – Contact us and choose your preferred package
  • Step 2 – Submit the required documents to our office
  • Step 3 – We handle the visa application on your behalf
  • Step 4 – Visa is usually processed within 2–4 weeks
  • Step 5 – Receive your travel documents and prepare for departure
